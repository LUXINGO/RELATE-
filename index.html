Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a business account? ➕
To create a business account, go to your profile page and click "Upgrade to Business". Fill in your business details and submit the application. An admin will review and approve your application within 24-48 hours.
How does the rating system work? ➕
New businesses start with 1.5⭐. The rating increases based on reviews. To go from 1.5⭐ to 2⭐, you need 10 five-star reviews. Each additional 0.5⭐ requires approximately 10 more five-star reviews. The formula is: New Rating = (Total Stars / Total Reviews). Admin can also manually adjust ratings.
What are the different business plans? ➕
We offer three plans: Basic ($1/month) - Limited leads, Elite ($5/month) - 50% visibility boost, Pro ($10/month) - 100% visibility boost and unlimited customer reach. Each plan lasts 30 days and requires renewal. You'll receive a warning 7 days before expiration.
How do I renew my business plan? ➕
Go to your business card and click "Renew Plan". Select your plan and complete payment via WhatsApp. Your account will be renewed for another 30 days from the renewal date.
How do I follow someone? ➕
Click the "Follow" button on any business card or user profile. You can only follow each user once. Click again to unfollow. Your following list is visible on your profile.
How do I search for users? ➕
Use the search bar on the home page to search by username, business name, or location. Results update in real-time as you type.
What happens if my account is deleted? ➕
If admin deletes your account, all your data (businesses, reviews, followers) will be permanently removed from the platform and will no longer appear in searches or on the home page.
